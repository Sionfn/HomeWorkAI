1
30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribed to HomeWorkAI Pro or Pro+ and are not satisfied for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 calendar days of your first payment. We will refund 100% of the amount charged, no questions asked.

This guarantee applies to your first payment on a new subscription. It does not apply to renewal payments after the initial 30-day window has passed.

2
Free Trial Cancellations

HomeWorkAI Pro and Pro+ come with a 3-day free trial. Your card is authorized when you start the trial but you are not charged until the trial period ends.

To avoid being charged, cancel your subscription before the 3-day trial period is up. You can cancel at any time by:

  • Going to your Account & Plan settings inside HomeWorkAI and clicking "Cancel Subscription"
  • Emailing us at support@homeworkaiapp.com and we will cancel it for you immediately

If you forget to cancel during the trial and are charged, you are still covered by our 30-day money-back guarantee on that first payment.

3
How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to support@homeworkaiapp.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • The email address associated with your HomeWorkAI account
  • The date of your purchase (optional — helps us find you faster)

You do not need to provide a reason, though feedback is always welcome. We will confirm your refund by email and process it within 5–10 business days. The refund will be returned to the original payment method used at checkout.

4
Renewals and Ongoing Subscriptions

After the initial 30-day guarantee window, subscription renewal payments are generally non-refundable. However, if you believe you were charged in error or forgot to cancel before a renewal, please contact us and we will review your case on a one-time basis.

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account & Plan settings or through the Stripe billing portal. Cancellation stops all future charges immediately. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

5
Yearly Subscriptions

Yearly Pro and Pro+ subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within the first 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, yearly subscriptions are non-refundable in full, but you may cancel to stop future renewals and retain access through the end of your paid year.

6
Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of abuse, including but not limited to repeatedly subscribing and requesting refunds, or accounts terminated for violating our Terms of Service.

The free plan does not involve any payment and therefore is not eligible for a refund.
