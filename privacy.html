Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to HomeWorkAI. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use HomeWorkAI:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google.
  • Usage data: The questions you ask and how often you use the service.
  • Payment information: Billing details are handled securely by Stripe. We never store your card number.
  • Device data: Browser type, device type, and general location for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the HomeWorkAI service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send important account and service updates
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

3. We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your homework questions and personal data stay private.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication (sign in with Google)
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • OpenAI — to power AI responses (questions are processed but not stored long-term)
  • Vercel — for hosting

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

HomeWorkAI is intended for users of all ages including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption to protect your data. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: gethomeworkai@gmail.com
